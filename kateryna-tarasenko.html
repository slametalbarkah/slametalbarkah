Kateryna Tarasenko

Kateryna Tarasenko (Ukrainian: Катерина Тарасенко; born 6 August 1987 in Dnipropetrovsk) is a Ukrainian rower. Along with Yana Dementyeva, she finished 7th in the women's double sculls at the 2008 Summer Olympics. Both of them won a gold medal at the 2012 Summer Olympics in the quadruple sculls event with Nataliya Dovgodko and Anastasiya Kozhenkova.

Tarasenko received Order of Merit in 2012.

Tarasenko is married to Ivan Futryk, an academic rower and European championship winner. They have two children.
