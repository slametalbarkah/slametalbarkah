Alessandro Covi

Alessandro Covi (born 28 September 1998) is an Italian cyclist, who currently rides for UCI WorldTeam UAE Team Emirates XRG. To date, the biggest win of his career is stage 20 of the 2022 Giro d'Italia, winning from the breakaway by a margin of 32 seconds to Domen Novak.
