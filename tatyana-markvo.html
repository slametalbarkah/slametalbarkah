Tatyana Markvo

Tatyana Mikhailovna Markvo (later Rakovshchik, Russian: Татьяна Михайловна Маркво (Раковщик), born 8 August 1941) is a retired Russian rower who won five medals in double and quad sculls at the European championships of 1966–1971. Her husband Leonid Rakovshchik also competed internationally in rowing.
