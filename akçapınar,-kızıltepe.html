Akçapınar, Kızıltepe

Akçapınar (Kurdish: Erban) is a neighbourhood in the municipality and district of Kızıltepe, Mardin Province in Turkey. The village is populated by Kurds of the Erbanî tribe and had a population of 494 in 2021.
