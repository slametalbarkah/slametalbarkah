Cyrtanthus falcatus

Cyrtanthus is a genus of perennial, herbaceous and bulbous plants in the family Amaryllidaceae, subfamily Amaryllidoideae.





== Taxonomy ==

Cyrtanthus is the sole genus in the African tribe Cyrtantheae.
