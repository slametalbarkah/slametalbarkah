DJ Shiwaawa

Terry Amoah, popularly known as DJ Shiwaawa, is a Ghanaian disc jockey. He won the Artiste DJ of the year in the 2018 edition of the Ghana DJ Awards while working with late Ebony.





== Career ==

DJ Shiwaawa started his career as a DJ in his mother's bar, Sweet City, in Nyakrom. He worked with Ruff Town Records whereby he was the personal DJ of late Ebony Reigns. He was also contract by VVIP to work with them on various projects alongside djing.
