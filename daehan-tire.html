Daehan Tire

Daehan Tire Industries Co Ltd. (대한타이어) is a tire company headquartered in Seoul, Korea and established in 1990. It has been supplying automobile inner tubes and flaps to Kumho Tires on an O.E.M basis since 1991. It also produced retread tires for trucks and buses for domestic and overseas markets since 1994.





== History ==

1990: Established Daehan Tube Industries Co Ltd.

1992: Acquired Korean Standards mark for automobile inner tubes.

1994: Became Daehan Tire Industries Co Ltd., Established facilities for retread tires.
