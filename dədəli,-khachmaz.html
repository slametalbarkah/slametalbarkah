Dədəli, Khachmaz

Dədəli (also, Dedeli) is a village and municipality in the Khachmaz Rayon of Azerbaijan. It has a population of 1,542. The municipality consists of the villages of Dədəli, Kiçik Baraxum, and Nağıoba.
