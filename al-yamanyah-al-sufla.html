Al-Yamanyah al-Sufla

Al-Yamanyah al-Sufla (Arabic: اليمانية السفلى) is a sub-district located in Jihanah District, Sana'a Governorate, Yemen. Al-Yamanyah al-Sufla had a population of 16850 according to the 2004 census.
